OUR VISION

Sahaj Path Knowledge Foundation, a not-for-profit organisation, believes that quality education is the foundation of sustainable development. The future talents, the builders of modern India in the coming days, remain scattered particularly in rural sector and they are to be spotted and nourished with necessary educational inputs.

BE A CONTENT DEVELOPER

The Foundation intend to develop quality contents and make them available over internet for use or download, by the students. Basically the syllabi developed by the WBBSE will be followed. Some contents for the proposed classes in different subjects are to be developed and stacked in the website. These pre-recorded audio files will cover the basic text and common queries including audio quiz to assess learning outcomes.

You may volunteer to develop such contents in your preferred area or of choice. Please contact for further details.

BE AN INTERN

The Foundation has already engaged a good number of tutors for attending the calls of the students according to their availability. In case you are having some time which can be utilised for a social cause, please feel free to contact the Foundation. You will love to experience the work of tutoring and mentoring humble students.
