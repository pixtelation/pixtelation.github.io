Welcome

"Little Buddy" aims at providing high quality Pre-school, Primary, Secondary and Sr. Secondary education with exceptional facilities and unique training methodology. Genius Kids is a part of group of Institutions, having its competency in educational field for more than 24 years. Little Buddy is built around the strong belief that the childhood of a little one is precious. It is meant to be enjoyed and cherished. Research and in-depth studies have proven that the first five years of childhood are the most formative. "Little Buddy" encourage parents to share their views, experiences through regular interactions, events, workshops and encourage visiting during school hours to have a sheer feeling of joy in observing the child's regular chores that is progressing the child's overall development.
